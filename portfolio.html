About

Who I Am

I'm Likubangwa Jay-Z Nyeliso — a BSc Computer Science student at the Namibia University of Science and Technology (NUST), graduating 2026, specializing in system and network administration.

I worked as a System Administrator and IT Officer at I-TECH Namibia, seconded to the Ministry of Health and Social Services under the PEPFAR digital health initiative. I was part of a national rollout of 250+ HP and Supermicro servers and Fortinet firewalls across healthcare facilities across Namibia.

I build and document hands-on lab environments covering Active Directory, Linux infrastructure, enterprise email, networking, and data centre design — turning real-world work into reproducible documentation.
